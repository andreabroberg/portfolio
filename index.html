About Me

I recently graduated from the Growth Marketing program at Berghs School of Communication. My way of working combines creativity with a data-driven approach, and I have a strong interest in what actually drives results in digital advertising and how small changes can make a real impact.

During my internship at the communications agency Appleberg, I worked with campaign-related tasks across digital platforms, including setting up and following up on campaigns as well as producing ads and content. I also worked with adapting messaging based on target audience, channel, and performance.

With a background in service, I’m used to working with customers in fast-paced environments and delivering under pressure.
