About Me

Recently graduated from the Growth Marketing program at Berghs School of Communication, I’m looking forward to starting my career within digital marketing and strategy.

With a background in service and internship experience in performance marketing at an agency, I’ve developed the ability to bring structure to complex work, manage multiple priorities, and collaborate effectively in team environments. Working in service has also taught me patience, strong communication skills, and how to stay focused and reliable in high pressure situations.

I’m motivated by understanding what truly drives results where opportunities are missed and how something that already works can be made even better. For me, decisions shouldn’t rely on gut feeling alone, but be supported by strategy, analysis, and data.

Recycle Life
